About Me (Why I can deliver)

Enrolled as an Advocate in 1998, I began my professional journey under the guidance of Senior Advocate Mr. G. Papi Reddy. I completed my Law Degree and LL.M. from University Law College, Bengaluru, and have practiced across Family Courts, Civil Courts, the High Court, and Tribunals.

I serve as Standing Counsel for Public Sector Companies and represent Life Insurance Companies, while consistently offering pro bono services to the poor and underprivileged. I am also a trained Mediator with the Karnataka Mediation Centre.

Since 2009, I have been running Geetha Raj & Associates, grounded in daily court practice realities and the real needs of advocates.

90-Day Implementation Plan

Institutional foundations and irreversible processes for sustained reform:

  • Welfare Access Systems: Helpdesks at Bar Associations + published claim-processing pathway with timelines.
  • Young Advocate Mentorship Calendar: Monthly training modules (court craft, drafting, ethics).
  • Women Advocate Support Protocol: Rapid-response mechanisms + safety protocols + leadership representation.
  • Insurance & Pension Framework Initiation: Consultations with insurers, actuaries, and Bar Associations for group medical insurance and pension models.
  • Legislative Advocacy: Formal Bar Council resolutions endorsing Fake Advocates Bill and Protection from Violence Bill + State Government engagement.
  • Mediation Readiness Programs: Settlement drafting + negotiation discipline for advocates.

By Day 90: Functioning welfare systems, structured training, safety protocols, insurance/pension frameworks under development, and active legislative engagement.
